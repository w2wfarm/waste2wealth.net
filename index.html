About Us

Waste2Wealth [W2W] transforms organic waste into valuable products using Black Soldier Fly (BSF) farming. Based in Syadrit, Umsning, we create animal feed and fertilizer, helping the environment and empowering our community.

Our Process

  1. Collect food waste
  2. Feed it to BSF larvae
  3. Harvest protein-rich larvae and organic fertilizer
  4. Sell products to farmers

Our Products

  • BSF Larvae: High-protein feed for animals
  • BSF Meal & Oil: Sustainable livestock feed
  • Organic Fertilizer (Frass): 100% natural

Impact

Recycled over 5,000 kg of waste, produced sustainable animal feed, supported 30+ farmers, and created 10+ jobs in our community.
